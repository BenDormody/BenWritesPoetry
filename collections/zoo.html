With Zoo as Background

Shifting Sands

Camouflaged creature,
His tie, nature knotted,
Adapted garb, suit the scene—
Masked Intentions
Empowers his elusive deviance.
Lucky me, my house has thick walls.
Elect; a term of comfort; leaves me,
Oblivious to adjacent dangers…
Now he's got my daughter's body.
